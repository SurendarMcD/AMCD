Hello McDonald’s Canada,

Like many of you, I took some downtime in August with my family; it was a chance to hit the road with my wife and kids, and stop at a few McDonald’s along the way. The 4-5-5
Plan to Win

The 4-5-5 Plan to Win for 2010-2014 has been the framework for our business plan that has seen the largest growth in the past 20 years for McDonald’s Canada. We have ahad an amazing success story and re-establishing a stronger connection with Canadians and strengthening the system. There were several turning points in exectuting our strategies. The Canadian revitilization story began with a real effort of Franchisees engagement and alignment and a commitment to reconnecting with Canadians via the overhaul of the marketing funding model (MFM).
Our Destination 2012 efforts resulted in system-wide modernization and capacity building plan. We launched the McCafe brand platform, re-energized our focus on breakfast and deployed via perfect executio with a “gold standard” approach. Food quality initiatives, broadening accessability with free wifi, increased payment methods and late night further connected with Canaidans. Our guests have felt the changes in our Brand. All our efforts drove improved Brand Health, last visit satisfaction and visit frequency resulting in all time high Guest Counts, Sales and Cashflow.
